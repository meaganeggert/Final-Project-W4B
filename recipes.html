Ingredients
DOUGH
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (or 2 ¼ tsps.) instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tbsps unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 large egg
FILLING
  • 3 tbsps unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar or packed brown sugar
ICING
  • 1 stick butter, softened
  • 1 8oz package cream cheese
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2-3 cups powdered sugar
Directions
  1. Whisk the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast together in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Combine milk, water, and butter together in a heatproof bowl. Heat until butter is melted and the mixture is warm to the touch (about 110F). Pour into the dry ingredients, add an egg, and use a stand mixer to stir until a soft dough forms.On a lightly floured surface, knead dough for 3 minutes. Place kneaded dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover loosely, and let rest for about 20 minutes.
  3. While dough is resting, combine the melted butter, sugar, and cinnamon for the filling.
  4. After 20 minutes, roll the dough out in a 14x8 inch rectangle. Use a spoon to spread the filling out all over the dough.
  5. Use a pizza or pastry wheel to cut 10-12 even strips from the rectangle. Roll the strips and place into a lightly greased 9-inch cake pan or pie dish.
  6. Tightly cover the rolls with plastic wrap and allow them to rise in a warm environment for 60-90 minutes.
  7. After the rolls have doubled in size, preheat the oven to 375°F. Bake for 25-28 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  8. While the rolls are cooling, make the icing:
    • Beat together the butter and cream cheese until light and fluffy (about 2-3 minutes).
    • Add in the vanilla and mix at a medium speed until thoroughly combined.
    • Set your mixer to low and slowly add in the powdered sugar.*
      *Start with 2 cups of powdered sugar and add slowly until desired sweetness is achieved
  9. Top the rolls with the cream cheese icing. Enjoy!
